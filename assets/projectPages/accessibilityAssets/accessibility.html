Accessibility and AI

UX Research | May 2021-September 2021 | Western Washington University

A UX research study on WWU computer science student's understanding of accessibility topics in relation to AI. The research seeks to gather data on students' experiences taking AI course and their knowledge of the connections between AI and accessibility topics in order to support and improve the development of AI and Accessibility courses at Western Washington University.

Access the paper (SIGCSE 2022): Exploration on Integrating Accessibility into an AI Course

My Role: UX Designer

Recruitment, Surveys, Semi-structured Interviews, Data Analysis and Synthesis, Paper Write-up

Methods:

Descriptive Surveys, Semi-structured Interviews, Data analysis
